A reversed copy of an original created in Lowell, Mass.,

This copy of the original "Annie" daguerreotype owned by Annie Richmond may have been made in the early 1850s for Poe's mother-in-law, Maria (Muddy) Clemm, who was then residing as a guest of Mrs. Richmond in Lowell.
