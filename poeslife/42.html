THE BOSTON ATLAS
Advertisement
October 7, 1845

This notice of the Boston Lyceum anniversary event on Thursday, October 16, 1845, states that "an address will be pronounced by Hon. CALEB CUSHING, and a Poem by EDGAR A POE Esq of New York" and that this will "be followed by Lectures on ... succeeding THURSDAY EVENING[S]" by the poet and abolitionist James Russell Lowell and the leading Transcendentalist philosopher of the day, Ralph Waldo Emerson, among others.
